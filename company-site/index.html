Building More Than Spaces — We Build Trust.

At Ratonga Property Services, we don’t just fix, build, and maintain—we create spaces where people can truly live, work, and thrive. From our roots in home renovations to over 350 successful projects across the North Island, we’ve become a trusted full-service partner for property care and construction in Wellington and Manawatū-Whanganui.

Our 20+ expert team—including electricians, plumbers, roofers, and carpenters—delivers everything from mould removal and meth decontamination to full renovations, fencing, roofing, and system upgrades. Every project is completed with precision, pride, and on-time delivery.

We’re fully licensed, insured, and committed to quality, adaptability, and clear communication—so you can trust us to get it right, no matter the challenge.
