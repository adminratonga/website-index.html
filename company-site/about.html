Company Overview: Ratonga Property Services

Reliable. Professional. Proven.

At Ratonga Property Services, we are more than builders and tradespeople—we are partners in creating safe, comfortable, and functional spaces where people live, work, and thrive. Since our inception, we have operated with a people-first philosophy, understanding that architecture is not just about structures, but about improving lives through thoughtful environments.

Founded with little more than a clear vision and a deep commitment to quality, Ratonga began as a small home renovation and maintenance business. Through steady growth, skilled craftsmanship, and the trust of our clients, we have evolved into a full-service construction and property services company, proudly serving the Wellington and Manawatū-Whanganui regions.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions across a broad range of service areas. Our promise to our clients is simple:

  • We restore vacant homes and bring them back to life
  • We decontaminate and rehabilitate damaged or unsafe properties
  • We maintain, clean, and paint with attention to every detail
  • We build fences, lay flooring, repair roofing, and upgrade electrical and plumbing systems
  • We deliver results—on time, on budget, and with pride

To date, we have successfully completed over 350 projects across the North Island, including meth decontamination for more than 40 properties and extensive renovation work for Housing NZ and private clients.

Our Team

Our strength lies in our people. With a team of over 20 experienced professionals, including electricians, plumbers, roofers, and carpenters, we offer seamless coordination across all project stages. This integrated approach ensures efficient timelines, high safety standards, and customised service delivery.

Whether it’s a single-room repair or a multi-dwelling development, every project is approached with the same level of care, precision, and respect for the client’s vision and values.

Resilience & Reliability

We understand that challenges are part of every project—be it supply chain disruptions, unpredictable weather, or changing project scopes. What sets us apart is our ability to adapt, communicate transparently, and remain committed through every stage of delivery.

Why Choose Ratonga

  • Proven delivery track record
  • Strong local knowledge and relationships
  • Fully licensed and insured professionals
  • Flexible pricing and tailored solutions
  • Deep experience across residential, commercial, and public sector projects

Let’s Build Something Better—Together

At Ratonga Property Services, we care about the outcomes that matter to you. We take pride in being a steady, dependable partner—from initial consultation to project handover.

We are here to build more than just buildings. We are here to build trust, quality, and lasting value—with heart, with hands, with Ratonga.
